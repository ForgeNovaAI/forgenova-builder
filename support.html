Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a demo?

You can request a demo by filling out our contact form. We’ll reach out within 1 business day.

Do I need technical skills to use ForgeNovaAI?

No. ForgeNova is a no-code builder designed for manufacturing teams. If you can describe your process, you can build an app.

What integrations are supported?

We support ERP/CMMS, REST/GraphQL APIs, and PLC/SCADA via OPC-UA/MQTT. Check the features page for details.
