What Is OEE and How to Improve It with No-Code Apps

Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) measures how well a manufacturing process performs relative to its full potential. It combines Availability, Performance, and Quality.

Quick formula

OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality

Typical losses to tackle

  • Unplanned downtime and changeovers (Availability)
  • Minor stops and slow cycles (Performance)
  • Defects and rework (Quality)
